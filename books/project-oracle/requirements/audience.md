# Audience

Primary audience:
- Adult readers who enjoy science-forward fiction and engineering problem-solving (fans of The Martian / Project Hail Mary).
- Engineers, scientists, and technically literate readers who appreciate plausible solutions and practical ingenuity.

Secondary audience:
- General adult readers who enjoy character-driven survival stories with humor and moral stakes.

Reading level and expectations:
- Accessible to general adult readers; technical content should be explained through the narrator's actions and kept engaging rather than academic.
