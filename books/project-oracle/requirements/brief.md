# Brief

Fiction — science-forward adventure in the style of The Martian and Project Hail Mary.

A post–World War II engineer is inexplicably pulled back in time and must use his technical skill, improvisation, and wartime experience to survive and influence a pivotal historical moment. Stranded with limited resources, he solves life-or-death problems with engineering ingenuity and wry humor while confronting the moral consequences of changing history. The novel blends rigorous problem-solving, human stakes, and dry, accessible humor.
